The idea behind it

What is biomagnetism?

A gentle therapy built on one elegant idea: the body has its own balance — and pairs of magnets can help it find its way back.

Biomagnetic pair therapy was developed in 1988 by Mexican physician Dr. Isaac Goiz Durán, and is practised today by therapists around the world.

The principle is simple: magnets are never used alone. They are always placed as a pair — one negative pole, one positive — on two matched points of the body identified during your scan. In the biomagnetism tradition, each pair gently supports the body's internal equilibrium where it's needed most.

There are no needles, no pressure, no undressing. Just stillness, warmth, and the quiet work of two poles finding balance.

The method

Where the magnet goes changes everything

Biomagnetic pair therapy is precise, not random. Every session follows the same careful arc — and each pair's placement is chosen specifically for you.

ii

Place

iii

You rest, fully clothed and deeply comfortable, for 20–40 minutes while the pairs gently support your body's return to equilibrium.

Honest answers

The questions everyone asks

The things people wonder but don't always ask out loud. Here's where Lea is completely straight with you.

Honest answer: biomagnetic pair therapy is an emerging complementary practice, not a proven medical treatment. The research is still developing and it isn't recognised as a cure for any condition. What Lea offers is a calming, non-invasive experience that many people find genuinely restful — offered alongside, never instead of, your regular medical care.

Most people feel deeply relaxed — many describe a warmth, a heaviness, or simply the first proper stillness they've had in weeks. Some feel a shift straight away; for others it's more subtle. There's no "right" way to experience it, and Lea will never tell you what you "should" feel.

Biomagnetism is gentle and non-invasive — no needles, no pressure, you stay fully clothed. It's designed to sit comfortably alongside conventional care. Always keep taking any medication and following your doctor's advice. If you have a pacemaker, are pregnant, or have a serious medical condition, please tell Lea beforehand and check with your doctor first.

It's completely individual. Some people come once for a reset; others prefer a short series spaced a week or two apart, then occasional top-ups. Lea will never push a package on you — after your first session you'll have a clear, no-pressure sense of what feels right for you.

Online sessions follow the same careful process — the assessment, the personal placement map, the guidance and aftercare — guided live by Lea over video. Many clients love that they can stay wrapped up on their own sofa, anywhere in the world. It's offered in both English and Portuguese.

More than okay — most of Lea's happiest clients started out unsure. You don't need to believe anything in advance. Come with an open mind, treat it as an hour of genuine rest, and let your own experience be the judge.
